Welcome

This was a love project that was started during our journeys around the United States. What started as just a hobbie for me, has become a passion that I hope to share. I have spent time looking for the hidden beauty in materials. Relying on the natural beauty that is around us each day to inspire these wristlett designs.


As we continue to look in to other crafts and project ideas, we hope that you will stay in touch with us for future updates.
